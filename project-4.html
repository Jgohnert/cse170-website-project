Presentation Website

For our fourth project, we met up on zoom and made this website to catagorize and keep track of the accomplishments we strived to achieve in order to complete our projects.

We had this great idea to make our presentation into a website, since all of us are taking a web fundamentals class and learning how to do web pages. We were able to share skills, and mentor each other in the process of creating this web page.

What we learned

We learned that our different inputs and ideas for a project like building a website makes it easier for us as a team to make something even better than what one person can come up with on their own.

We also learned new skills when programming together, and that we were able to teach each other along the way.
