For our second project, we were ushers for one of the devotionals.

We had so much fun sharing our skills with others while doing service. Cleaning our friends apartment and ushering for Devotional were very self-rewarding experiences. What we learned

We learned that when we devide the work as a team, we can get the job done much faster than if we were to do it ourselves.

We also learned that we can better manage our job when working together, because we understand that we as a team want to succeed, and by having this mindset, we are able to stay motivated in the work.
